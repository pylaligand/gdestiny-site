We're excited to announce our first ever clan PvP tournament!

The tournament will take place between November 12th and December 3rd. Sorry Playstation folks, but we'll focus on Xbox for the first iteration and will expand in the future.

This is a 4v4 team-based tournament with a group stage followed by an elimination stage. Matches are played as best of 3 games, with games played in the following playlists: Skirmish, Salvage, and Elimination. For each game, a map and a modifier (see below) will be assigned by the organization.

The group stage determines seeding for the elimination stage. The final match will be played as best of 5, as will the match for third place

Game Rules

No particular restriction will be imposed on equipment and subclass perks outside of modifiers. Game modifiers will be similar to regular Destiny modifiers. Sample modifiers:
  • Melting Pot: teams must have at least one subclass of each element
  • Praise the Sun: all players must use a solar subclass
  • Electric Boogaloo: all players must use an arc subclass
  • The Dark Side: all players must use a void subclass
  • Multi-tool: maximum one weapon of each type per team (one person cannot use heavy if teams have 4 players)
  • Stormtroopers: all team members must pick the same weapon types
  • Standard issue: no exotic equipment allowed
  • Inferno: self-explanatory
