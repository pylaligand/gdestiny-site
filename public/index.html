Welcome Guardians

Welcome to the revamped gDestiny clan site. Let us tell you a little about our clan...

We are a company clan of very dedicated Destiny players who love to play, grind for the perfect god roll weapon, and number crunch perks. We're a clan that spans both the Xbox and Playstation platforms, with active members in both. If PvE is your cup of team, we love to raid. If PvP is your thing, then let's shoot for the lighthouse together.

If you're a prospective Guardian interested in joining, then join the clan, join us on Slack for group chats (Destiny, Overwatch and video games in general), and see you at The Tower.
